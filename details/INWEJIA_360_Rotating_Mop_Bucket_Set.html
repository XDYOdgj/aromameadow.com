Smart Design for Cleaner Results

Designed to make your cleaning routine smarter, the INWEJIA 360° Rotating Mop & Bucket Set keeps your floors spotless with its dual-chamber water separation system. This ensures clean water stays fresh and uncontaminated, so you can mop without worrying about spreading dirt or grime.
