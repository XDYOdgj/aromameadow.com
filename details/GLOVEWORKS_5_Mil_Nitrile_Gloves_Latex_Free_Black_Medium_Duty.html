GLOVEWORKS 5 Mil Nitrile Gloves

A durable, latex-free alternative for those who need reliable protection in high-usage environments.

Why Choose GLOVEWORKS?

These gloves are engineered for superior chemical resistance, shielding your hands from harmful substances like grease, gasoline, and other petroleum-based materials. The extra-thick 5 mil nitrile construction ensures effective protection without sacrificing comfort, even during long shifts.

Crafted in a sleek black color, they not only look professional but also minimize the visibility of dirt and grime. This makes them an excellent choice for both work and everyday use, where hygiene and appearance matter.

With a micro-rough textured surface, GLOVEWORKS gloves offer enhanced grip and control, helping you handle tools and tasks with precision. This design maintains your dexterity, making them ideal for sensitive and demanding applications.

Perfect for a Range of Industries

Whether you're in the automotive industry, working in a restaurant, or maintaining your home, these gloves are built to meet your needs. Their versatility extends to a variety of tasks, from oil changes and equipment maintenance to food preparation and cleaning.

Being latex-free, they are a safe alternative for individuals with allergies, providing comfortable wear that rivals traditional latex gloves. This makes them a trusted choice for professionals and everyday users alike.
