SAMMART 7.5L Collapsible Dishpan

Looking for a reliable and space-saving dishpan that adapts to your lifestyle? The SAMMART 7.5L Collapsible Dishpan is the perfect choice for both short-term and long-term use. With a detachable draining plug, it ensures easy drainage while keeping your workspace clean and dry. The lightweight yet sturdy construction makes it ideal for everyday cleaning or on-the-go adventures.

Key Features

  • Large 7.5L capacity for efficient dish washing and draining
  • BPA-free, durable material that's safe for use and dishwasher-ready
  • Integrated draining plug for quick and easy water removal
  • Foldable design that collapses flat, saving valuable storage space
  • Sturdy four legs for stability, whether at home or on the trail

Why Choose This Dishpan?

Whether you're prepping for a family dinner or cleaning up after a BBQ in the backyard, this versatile dishpan fits perfectly into your routine.
