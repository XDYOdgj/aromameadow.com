ARTIPOLY 5-12ft Squeegee with 20ft Reach Window Washer

Elevate your cleaning performance with this 2-in-1 professional window cleaner tool. Whether you're tackling large windows, mirrors, or even solar panels, the ARTIPOLY is your reliable partner for spotless results every time.

Effortless Cleaning, Enhanced Efficiency

The ARTIPOLY window cleaner tool is built to simplify your cleaning routine and boost productivity. Its 14-inch rubber blade is designed for powerful cleaning, effectively removing dirt, grime, and water spots without leaving behind any unsightly streaks. The chenille cleaning agent adds an extra layer of effectiveness, making it easy to clean windows and other glass surfaces quickly and thoroughly.

Durable and Versatile Design

Engineered for both indoor and outdoor use, the ARTIPOLY squeegee features a robust alloy extension pole that can be extended to 20ft. This telescoping squeegee pole is a must-have for anyone who values efficiency and quality in their cleaning tools. The ARTIPOLY is not just a tool — it's an investment in a cleaner, faster, and more enjoyable cleaning experience.
