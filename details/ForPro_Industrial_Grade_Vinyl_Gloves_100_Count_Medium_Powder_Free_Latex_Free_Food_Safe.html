ForPro Disposable Vinyl Gloves

Engineered for strength, comfort, and safety, ForPro gloves are the perfect choice for everyday use in food service, healthcare, and industrial settings. They combine durable materials with a sleek, user-friendly design to meet the needs of professionals and everyday users alike.

Key Features

  • Industrial-grade vinyl construction for heavy-duty use
  • Powder-free and latex-free formulation for sensitive skin
  • Textured palm and finger design for superior grip and slip resistance
  • Food-safe and suitable for all food-handling tasks
  • Ambidextrous design for easy one-handed use

Why Choose ForPro?

When you're working in a kitchen, handling medical equipment, or managing a manufacturing line, protection is essential. ForPro gloves ensure that you can complete your tasks with confidence, offering a secure and reliable barrier against moisture, chemicals, and germs.

The textured finish on the palms and fingers ensures a strong grip, even in wet or greasy conditions. This makes them ideal for use in a variety of environments where dexterity and control are needed.

Unlike traditional gloves, ForPro's powder-free design eliminates the risk of skin irritation or allergic reactions, making them a safer, more comfortable option for both professionals and individuals with sensitivities.

Each pack includes 100 gloves, stored in a convenient dispenser box for quick access and easy storage. Whether you're hosting an event, managing a workspace, or just doing daily chores, these gloves are ready to use whenever and wherever you need them.

Perfect for Every Task

From restaurant staff to home users and healthcare workers, ForPro gloves provide reliable protection without compromising comfort or usability. Their clear design allows for better visibility, which is especially useful when working with fragile items or in precise environments.

With a thickness of 2.75 mil on the palm and 3.9 mil on the fingers, they offer a perfect balance of flexibility and durability. This ensures that they can be used in a wide range of applications, including household cleaning, painting, and more.

Cost-Effective and Reliable

Packed in sets of 10, ForPro gloves are an affordable solution for frequent use. They are perfect for businesses looking to maintain hygiene standards without overspending, and for individuals who need reliable protection for day-to-day tasks.

These disposable gloves are non-sterile but still meet high standards of hygiene and safety, making them a practical choice for everyday wear. You can rely on them to perform consistently and safely, whether in a professional setting or at home.
